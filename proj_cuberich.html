Cuberich

A project by Clemens Rögner

Cuberich is a real-time rendering demo which I programmed together with Christian Hirsch for the course Echtzeitgraphik (real time rendering) using DirectX 11. It features several visual effects, poor models (and textures) and is the second graphical application me and my friend ever implemented.

General

Best Demo Award Triangulated Milka

Me and my friend actually won the best demo award in that semster, due to its effect overkill :D

Effects Three would have been enough

  • Glow
  • Variance Shadow Maps (regular & cube mapped for point lights)
  • Screen Space God Rays
  • Lens Flare
  • HDR rendering with Reinhardt Tone Mapper
  • Defeered Shading
  • Normal Mapping
  • Horizon Based SSAO
  • Environment Mapping for Reflections
  • Water Shader
